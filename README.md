**MockMate** is a realistic AI-powered mock interview platform that simulates real interview pressure, evaluates your answers deeply, and tells you exactly where you stand.  

---

## ðŸŽ¯ Features

- **Context-Aware AI Interviews**: Questions generated based on role, experience, job description, interview focus, and target companies.  
- **Voice + Text Answering**: Answer naturally using voice (with transcript) or type structured responses. Maximum 3 voice attempts per question.  
- **Real Interview Pressure**: Full-screen mode, live timer, limited retries, and auto-end when time runs out.  
- **Supports All Interview Types**: Technical, HR, managerial, finance, product, across intern to senior levels.  
- **Deep AI Evaluation**: Overall feedback + question-wise analysis (scores, strengths, gaps, ideal answers).  
- **Fitment & Recommendation**: AI evaluates your suitability and gives hiring-level recommendations.

---

## ðŸ›  Interview Journey

1. **Configure Your Interview**  
   - Choose role & interview type (Technical, HR, Managerial)  
   - Set experience level & duration  
   - Provide job description, interview focus & target companies  

2. **Answer Under Real Conditions**  
   - Full-screen environment  
   - Type answers or record voice  

3. **Interview Evaluation**  
   - Overall score & hiring recommendation  
   - Strengths & improvement areas  
   - Question-wise scores, feedback & ideal answers  

4. **Improve with Clarity**  
   - Understand failures and gaps  
   - Targeted improvement for your desired companies  
   - Re-practice with smarter focus
