# MockMate

**MockMate** is a realistic AI-powered mock interview platform that simulates real interview pressure, evaluates your answers deeply, and tells you exactly where you stand.  

Built to help job seekers practice interviews under real-world conditions, MockMate offers voice and text answering, context-aware questions, AI-powered evaluation, and actionable feedback.  

---

## Features

- **Context-Aware AI Interviews**  
  Questions are dynamically generated based on role, experience, job description, interview focus, and target organizations.

- **Voice + Text Answering**  
  Answer naturally using voice (with transcript) or type structured responses. Voice attempts are limited to simulate real interview pressure.

- **Real Interview Pressure**  
  Full-screen mode, live timer, limited retries, and auto-end when time runs out.

- **Supports All Interview Types**  
  Technical, HR, managerial, finance, product, and more, across intern to senior levels.

- **Deep AI Evaluation**  
  Provides overall feedback plus question-wise analysis including score, strengths, gaps, and ideal answers.

- **Fitment & Recommendation**  
  AI evaluates your suitability for the role and provides hiring-level recommendations.

---

## Interview Journey

1. **Configure Your Interview**  
   - Choose role: Software Engineer, Finance Manager, HR, etc.  
   - Select interview type: Technical, HR, Managerial  
   - Set experience level & duration  
   - Provide job description, interview focus & target companies  

2. **Answer Under Real Conditions**  
   - Full-screen interview environment  
   - Type answers or record voice (max 3 attempts per question)  

3. **Interview Evaluation**  
   - Get overall score & hiring recommendation  
   - View strengths & improvement areas  
   - Question-wise scores, feedback & ideal answers  

4. **Improve with Clarity**  
   - Understand exactly where you failed  
   - Learn what to improve for your target companies  
   - Practice again with smarter focus  

---

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend:** React, TailwindCSS, Shadcn, Lucide-react, Redux Toolkit, Vite  
- **Backend:** Node.js, Express.js, MongoDB  
- **AI & Voice Processing:** OpenAI, AssemblyAI  
- **Utilities:** Express-validator, Cloudinary, Multer, Bcrypt, JWT, Cors
